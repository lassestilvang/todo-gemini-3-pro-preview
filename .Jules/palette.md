## 2024-03-12 - Added ARIA Label to Calendar Add Task Button\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons revealed on hover within complex components (like the calendar day grid) must have explicit `aria-label` attributes to ensure they are accessible to screen readers, especially when they represent important actions like adding a task.\n**Action:** Always verify that interactive elements relying solely on icons include descriptive, visually-hidden text or `aria-label`s.
